--- layout: single title: "Projects" permalink: /projects/ author_profile: true redirect_from: - /talks/ share: false --- Selected research and engineering projects, with an emphasis on multi-robot systems and 3D scene understanding. ## Research Projects ### Multi-UAV Mesh Networking and Localization System for Live-line Safety Operations *National Undergraduate Innovation Project, project lead.* - Built a multi-UAV mesh network and improved OLSR routing with ETX-based link quality to reduce dropouts. - Integrated RTK + UWB fusion and ESO dual-loop disturbance rejection for centimeter-level positioning and stable hovering. - Implemented ROS + AirSim simulation and hardware validation. ### DreamVGGT: VGGT-Guided Generative 3D Gaussian Splatting - Single-image 3D scene customization with geometric-semantic alignment. - Related paper and demo: [DreamVGGT](/publication/dreamvggt/). ### Distributed Multi-Robot Multi-Target Search and Tracking - Joint exploration, coverage, and tracking in unknown non-convex environments. - Related paper and demo: [SSAT](/publication/ssat/). ### FPGA-Based Football Match Analysis System (ZYNQ-7020) - Built a heterogeneous FPGA+ARM pipeline for real-time football analytics (Jiangsu “苏超” league scenario). - YOLOv8 pruning/quantization for lightweight detection; PyQt5 UI for visualization and replay. - ~10 fps, <100 ms latency; supports player/ball tracking, event detection, and radar-view tactics. - Report: [PDF](/files/projects/project-report-1107.pdf) ### FPGA-Based Elderly Fall Detection (ZYNQ-7020 + YOLOv7) - Embedded fall-detection system on ZYNQ-7020 with YOLOv7 pruning/quantization. - Hardware–software co-design: convolution acceleration on PL, control and classification on PS. - Targets low-latency, low-power monitoring for home and clinical scenarios. - Report: [PDF](/files/projects/fpga-report-11-11.pdf) ### ALOHA Policy Reproduction (In Progress) - Reproducing the ALOHA bimanual manipulation policy: data pipeline, training stack, and benchmarking tasks. - Focus on teleoperation data collection, imitation learning, and sim-to-real evaluation. - Integrating perception (RGB-D/3D) and control interfaces for hardware experiments. ## Competitions and Engineering Practice - RoboCup China Finals (National 1st Prize).
